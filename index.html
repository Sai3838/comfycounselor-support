Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I use Comfy Counselor?
    Simply open the app and start chatting with our therapy chatbot. It's designed to provide emotional support and guidance.
  • How can I purchase unlimited messaging?
    Go to the settings in the app and select the option to purchase unlimited messaging for a week.
  • What should I do if the app crashes?
    Try restarting the app. If the problem persists, contact us at support@comfycounselor.com.

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues, try the following steps:

  1. Restart the app.
  2. Check for updates in the App Store.
  3. Ensure your device is running the latest version of its operating system.
